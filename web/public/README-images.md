# Images for the Qafila landing page

Drop generated PNGs here (they are served from `/`). The site works without
them (there is a CSS/SVG fallback), but the hero render is what makes it sing.

## Required (1)
- **hero-lantern.png** — a glowing brass fanous (Arabic lantern) resting on a
  pale marble pedestal, soft smoke, warm gold light, on a transparent or ivory
  (`#F5F1E8`) background. Square-ish, ~1200×1200, PNG with transparency preferred.

### ChatGPT / image-gen prompt
> A luxurious 3D render of a glowing golden Arabic fanous lantern on a white
> marble pedestal, warm gold light spilling from the lattice, faint smoke,
> soft studio lighting, ivory background, premium fintech product shot,
> photorealistic, centered, square composition.

## Optional polish
- **hero-lantern.png** can instead be a transparent-background cutout for the
  cleanest look against the ivory hero.
- Feature icons currently use Phosphor glyphs; no images needed there.
